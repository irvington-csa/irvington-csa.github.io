Special orders

In addition to our weekly share basket, there are special order items available each week for purchase, such as, eggs, maple syrup, beef, honey, flour, flowers, etc. Special orders are placed by responding to the weekly email.

Please note that we may be updating the special order process for the 2023 season. A new price list for special orders is forthcoming from the farmer. Please stay tuned! We’ll share updates as soon as we can.

We use PayPal for special orders (no checks or cash). We’ll add a $1 service fee to each special order to cover PayPal charges to the farmer.

You can access Levi Fisher’s PayPal account three ways:

  1. twincrookfarm@gmail.com
  2. Levi Fisher
  3. 765-592-6082

Please use the “send to a friend” option, and in the notes section, please add the date, last name, and what you’re paying for.
